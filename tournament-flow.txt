** for reader's reference only

how APU runs the tennis tournament:
- all matches are mixed-gender singles
- all matches are held in May (fixed dates)
- player withdrawals = immediate substitute, no rescheduling

qualifying
- 1v1 based on rank and order of register
- 24 out of 48 will advance

round-robin
- the 24 qualified players are divided into 6 groups (4 per group)
- every player will play against each person within the group
- the player with most wins from each group advances (total: 6 finalists)

knockout rounds
- 6 players play in brackets (players with top 2 ranks start at semi-finals)
- final winner is determined
